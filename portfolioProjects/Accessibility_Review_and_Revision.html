Accessibility Review and Revision

Project Lead - Designer

Article detailing this process was published by American Library Association's journal: College and Research Libraries in their January 2023 issue : Volume 84, Issue 1. Writing the article requiered several revision drafts to elaborate on the process the article describes. After all revisions were completed and after communication with the publisher, the article was approved as a feature article for January's upcoming issue.

  • Invented a systematic review process for existing and future online tutorials
  • Feature length article displayed on the College and Research Libraries home page
  • Researched accessibility standards and aggregated them into a single review process
  • Unmet standards were compiled onto a list of updates to be made with explicit instructions
  • Instructions for unmet standards ordered from most severe to least severe and completed in that order
